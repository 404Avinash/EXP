Intro to Culinary Arts

Course Snapshot

This course teaches fundamental cooking techniques, kitchen safety, and recipe development. Expect hands-on labs and taste tests! Pro tip: always season to taste. Symbols: & (and), ° (degrees).

Glossary (Description List)

Braise
To cook slowly in liquid after browning.
Blind bake
Partially or fully baking a crust before filling.

Final exam: practical cooking challenge & recipe write-up.
